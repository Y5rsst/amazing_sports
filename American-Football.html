9. Rugby (American-Football)



With an estimated 400 million fans, American football is gaining popularity globally. Its explosive action and tactical gameplay have attracted viewers around the world. Rugby is a sport that combines elements of football, soccer, and American football. It requires speed, agility, strength, teamwork, and stamina. If you're new to rugby, focus on building these skills through regular practice and training. It's also important to learn the basic rules of the game and understand the different player positions. The best way to start your rugby journey is by joining a local league or finding a training club in your area. This will help you learn from experienced players and improve your skills with their guidance.
  • Estimated fans: 400 million.
  • Regional popularity: Europe, Africa, Asia, America, and Australia.
  • This famous American sport is gaining popularity in many regions of the world.
