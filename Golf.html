10. Golf



Golf is a popular activity for millions, with an estimated fan base of 450 million people. The sport's appeal is genuinely global, with fans in Europe, Asia, America, and Canada enjoying both its serene and competitive sides. Golf draws people of various ability levels, from casual players to devoted pros. Golf has become very popular around the world for several reasons. One big reason is that golf is a great way to get out into nature and enjoy the beautiful scenery while also getting some exercise. This combination of enjoying the outdoors and staying active makes golf appealing to many people.
  • Estimated fans: 450 million.
  • Popular regions include Europe, Asia, the United States, and Canada.
  • A cherished hobby enjoyed by roughly 60 million people each year.
